RY MPANJAKAM-BONINAHITRA

1
Ry Mpanjakam-boninahitra
He ! midera Anao,
Izahay 'zay very lavitra
Fa naverinao

ISAN'ANDININY :
Ekeonao, ry Ray, (ry Ray)
Ekeonao, ry Ray, (ry Ray)
Ny saotra aterinay
Ho mandrakizay.

2

3
