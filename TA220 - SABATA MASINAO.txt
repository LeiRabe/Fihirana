SABATA MASINAO

1
Sabata Masinao,
Andriamanitray,
No nitsaharanao,
Ka itsaharanay.
Sabata Masinao,
Ry Tompo tianay,
Izao anio izao
Ka ifalianay.

ISAN'ANDININY :
Sabata Masinao,
Andriamanitray,
No nitsaharanao,
Ka itsaharanay.

2
Sabata Masinao,
Jehovah Tompo soa.
No voatahinao
Ka maminay tokoa.
Sabata Masinao, sns.

3
Sabata Masinao,
Ry Ray malalanay,
Nohamasininao
Ka hamasininay.
Sabata Masinao, sns.

4
Sabata Masinao,
Ry Jeso Tomponay,
No tena andronao
Ka ivavahanay.
Sabata Masinao, sns.
