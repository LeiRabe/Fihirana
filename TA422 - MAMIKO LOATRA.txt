MAMIKO LOATRA

1
Mamiko loatra
Ny eo akaikinao, ry Ray.
Lavitra sento,
Tsy misy manjo.
Eo 'zany 'zaho
Hitoetra mandrakizay,
Anilanao, ry Ray,
'Zaho sambatra.

2
Akaikinao,
Mangidy toa mamy ;
Eo 'zany foko
Ampoky ny soa.
Ny fahoriana,
loza izay ho tamy,
Anilanao
Dia tonga zava-tsoa.

3
Akaikinao
No misy famonjena ;
He! very toky
Raha hilaozanao.
Lavitra Anao
Milofika ny tena.
Tariho, Ray,
Eo akaikinao.
