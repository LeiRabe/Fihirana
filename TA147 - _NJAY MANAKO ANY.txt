'NJAY MANAKO ANY

1
'Njay namako any
Feo miantso hoe :
Mihainoa ry tany,
Jeso ho avy re !

ISAN'ANDININY :
Maran'ata, Marana ata !
Tompo ô ! avia
Miandry ela ny olonao
O ! avia, avia !

2
Indro avy iIzy
Amin-drahona !
Faly sy mihoby
'Zay niomana.
Marana ata, sns.

3
Mihorohoro mafy
'Reo tsy vonona,
Miantso vatolampy
Hialofana.
Marana ata, sns.

4
'Zaho, ry Mpamonjy
Tano ho Anao.
Mba ho voavonjy,
Ka hidera Anao.
Marana ata, sns.
